Who We Are

Built On Grit,
Run On Results

Forge Fitness opened its doors nine years ago with one simple idea: fitness should be honest. No fads, no empty promises — just proven training methods, real equipment, and coaches who actually know what they're doing.

Our mission is simple — help every member walk in stronger than they walked out, one honest session at a time, in a space where beginners and lifters train side by side without judgement.
